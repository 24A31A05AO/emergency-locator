👉 Why Use Emergency Resource Locator?

During emergencies, finding the right help quickly can save lives. This platform is designed to provide fast and reliable access to essential services. Whether it's medical assistance, law enforcement, or basic needs like pharmacies, everything is just a click away.

Built using modern web technologies and real-time mapping, this system ensures accuracy, speed, and ease of use.

Stack: Leaflet.js · OpenStreetMap · OSRM Routing · Google Fonts
